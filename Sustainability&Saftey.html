Safety & Sustainability Statement

At Hickes Association Inc., as specialists in water waste treatment construction, our commitment to safety and sustainability is paramount. Recognizing the unique challenges posed by projects in this vital sector, we place an unwavering emphasis on ensuring the well-being of our workforce and the preservation of our environment. Safety is not just a priority; it is a fundamental aspect of our operations. Our team undergoes specialized training to address the intricacies of water waste treatment construction, and we strictly adhere to the guidelines set forth by the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) in Pennsylvania. By incorporating OSHA-compliant practices, we uphold the highest standards of safety, mitigating risks and fostering a secure environment for our team members. In line with our commitment to sustainability, we understand the critical role water waste treatment plays in preserving our natural resources. Our construction practices prioritize eco-friendly materials and innovative technologies to minimize environmental impact. From the initial project planning stages to the final construction phase, we integrate sustainable solutions that not only meet regulatory requirements but also contribute positively to the overall health of the ecosystem. Hickes Association Inc. is not just a construction company; we are stewards of safety and sustainability in water waste treatment projects.
